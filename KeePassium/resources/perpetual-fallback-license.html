Perpetual fallback license

With perpetual fallback license, you can use some premium features even if your subscription has expired.

Perpetual license covers all the premium features that were followed by a 1-year subscription (or 12 consecutive monthly payments). In other words, after you have been subscribed for a year or more, you get a perpetual license for the year-old version of the full app. Whenever you renew your subscription, you extend the perpetual license.

How is this different from other subscriptions?

  • Classic subscriptions: you rent a premium service. Once you stop paying, you cannot use the service anymore.
  • KeePassium subscription: you buy a premium license on a schedule. Once you stop paying, you get to keep the premium features you purchased.

This protects you from unjustified price increases, stalled development and unresponsive support. At the same time, a steady revenue stream enables us to improve and maintain the app over the years.

Frequently asked questions

What if I subscribe for less than 12 months?
You will get full access to the app while your subscription is active. To get a perpetual license, you need to subscribe for at least 12 months.

Would I get perpetual premium support?
Premium support is available only to active subscribers.

Why the perpetual license does not include the latest version?
This way customers know which features they would lose if they cancel the subscription. As a result, more people would chose to continue the subscription. With more subscribers, we can keep the subscription rates lower.
